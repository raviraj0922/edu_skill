Course Modules

Module 1: Introduction to Retail Basics and Focus on Customer Satisfaction

  • An overview of Retail Sales: Industry overview, career trajectories, and primary duties.
  • Customer Journey Mapping: Gaining insight into the many points of interaction and areas of dissatisfaction experienced by customers.
  • Developing Rapport and Enhancing Communication Abilities: Enhancing listening skills, employing effective questioning strategies, and establishing trust.

Module 2: Digital Age Sales

  • Comprehending online retail platforms and customer behavior in the context of e-commerce and m-commerce.
  • Social Selling Strategies: Harnessing the power of social media to promote brand advocacy and generate leads.
  • Adapting to the advancements in payment technologies: Mobile wallets and contactless payments.

Module 3: Product Understanding and Storytelling

  • Using storytelling techniques to create persuasive narratives that establish a strong connection with customers in sales.
  • Developing Product Expertise: Acquiring comprehensive understanding of product characteristics, advantages, and distinctiveness.
  • Upselling and cross-selling techniques refer to strategies aimed at maximizing the value that customers bring to a business.

Module 4: Omnichannel retail and integration

  • Integrating physical and digital channels to ensure a smooth and cohesive customer experience across various platforms.
  • Comprehending and utilizing fulfillment options: Click & Collect, BOPIS, and Ship-from-Store.
  • Using In-Store Technology: Interactive kiosks, digital signage, and augmented reality/virtual reality applications.

Module 5: Sales and Personalization Data Analytics

  • Retail Data Analytics: Analyzing important data and understanding customer insights.
  • Personalization Strategies: Employing customer data to deliver tailored recommendations and promotions.
  • A/B Testing and Sales Optimization: Employing data to enhance sales techniques and enhance conversion rates.

Module 6: Sales Negotiation and Closing Techniques

  • Negotiation Strategies: Employing ethical negotiation strategies and fostering mutually beneficial outcomes.
  • Objection Handling: Dealing with client issues in a confident and efficient manner.
  • Securing Sales Commitments: Effective Techniques for Closing the Sale.

Module 7: Customer Loyalty and Networking

  • Developing Customer Loyalty Programs: Tactics for fostering repeat patronage and customer endorsement.
  • Advanced Communication & Relationship Management: Enhancing communication skills to effectively interact with customers of different personalities.
  • Utilizing CRM Systems: Using Customer Relationship Management software to oversee interactions and cultivate loyalty.

Module 8: The Future of Retail and Growth Mindset

  • Investigating emerging retail trends such as artificial intelligence (AI), personalized shopping experiences, and voice-activated commerce.
  • Developing the ability to adapt and continuously learn: Fostering an attitude of progress in an ever-changing industry.
  • Establishing SMART goals and constructing a career trajectory in sales: Defining objectives for achieving success and advancing professionally.


Final Assessment:
  • MCQ Based Online Assessment 120 Questions (180 Minutes)
  • Quizzes, assignments, role-playing exercises, and a final project to demonstrate acquired skills.
Additional:
  • Industry professionals will deliver guest lectures on cutting-edge retail methods.
  • Excursions to see cutting-edge retail stores or observe the deployment of advanced technology.
  • Opportunities to connect with experienced retail professionals for networking purposes.
