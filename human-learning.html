Learning Aims

  • Acquire a thorough comprehension of the fundamental principles of traditional retail supply chain management.
  • Examine the effects of disruptive technologies, such as automation, artificial intelligence, and blockchain, on retail supply networks.
  • Discover cutting-edge strategies for predicting future demand, optimizing stock control, and efficiently fulfilling orders across many sales channels.
  • Acquire expertise in constructing flexible and robust supply chains.
  • Comprehend the significance of implementing sustainable practices in the retail supply chain.
