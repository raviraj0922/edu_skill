Course Modules

Module 1: Fundamental principles and concepts of Retail Supply Chain

  • An overview of retail supply chains
  • Essential components of retail supply chain management include procurement, warehousing, shipping, and other related operations.
  • Strategies for managing inventory
  • Methods for predicting demand

Module 2: Disruptive technologies in the retail industry

  • The utilization of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) in the management of retail supply chains.
  • Robotics and automation in the context of warehousing and fulfillment.
  • Blockchain technology enabling enhanced transparency and traceability throughout supply chains.
  • Supply chain architecture: Omnichannel retail to integrates different channels, such as online and physical stores, to provide a seamless shopping experience for customers.

Module 3: Forecasting innovation and inventory management to support omnichannel retail operations.

  • Utilizing big data analytics to predict future demand
  • Strategic foresight and mitigation of potential risks
  • Methods for optimizing inventory that adapt to changing conditions
  • Enhanced monitoring and management of inventory via many channels

Module 4: Omnichannel Fulfillment

  • Developing a comprehensive and integrated approach to managing the flow of goods and services across several channels.
  • Distributed order fulfillment (DOF) models
  • Click-and-collect and buy-online-pickup-in-store (BOPIS) tactics
  • Optimization of last-mile delivery

Module 5: Constructing Agile and Resilient Supply Chains

  • Strategies for managing risks in the supply chain
  • Establishing and fostering strategic alliances and cooperation with suppliers
  • Flexibility and adaptation in the supply chain
  • Deployments of agile supply chains

Module 6: Sustainability within the retail supply chain

  • Green supply chain practices, such as sustainable packaging and ethical sourcing.
  • Minimizing the environmental impact of logistical operations by decreasing carbon emissions
  • Efficient and environmentally-friendly alternatives for delivering goods to their final destinations
  • The significance of sustainability in fostering client loyalty


Final Assessment:
  • MCQ Based Online Assessment 120 Questions (180 Minutes)
  • Case studies and group projects centered on the practical application of cutting-edge solutions to actual retail problems.
  • Individual assignments and quizzes
  • Final project or presentation demonstrating a suggested cutting-edge retail supply chain strategy.
Additional:
  • Industry professionals will be presenting as guest speakers, with a specific focus on innovative retail enterprises.
  • Guest lectures will be delivered by industry experts who are actively involved in cutting-edge retail supply chains.
  • Visits to prominent retail companies that demonstrate cutting-edge methods.
  • Networking opportunities with professionals from the sector.
  • Software and tools: Demand forecasting software, Warehouse management system (WMS).
