Target Audience: Higher Secondary Passout, Undergraduate

This curriculum gives students the abilities and information they need to succeed as customer service representatives in the fast-paced retail industry. We'll look at cutting-edge methods that are revolutionizing the market and provide you the tools you need to provide outstanding client experiences.

Program Length : 6 Weeks (90 Hours)

Learning Aims:

  • Learn the essentials of providing retail customer service.
  • Utilize cutting-edge technologies to improve relationships with customers.
  • Put into practice efficient communication techniques for a varied clientele.
  • Handle new developments in retail, including as personalization and multichannel experiences.
  • Gaining problem-solving abilities will help you effectively handle client concerns.

Modules of the Course:

Module 1: The Innovation Imperative and Retail Fundamentals

  • The three pillars of customer service excellence are rapport-building, communication strategies, and fundamental values.
  • The retail environment is changing due to the influence of technology, shifting consumer demands, and the growth of internet purchasing.
  • Innovation in retail: investigating trends such as social selling, chatbots driven by AI, and AR/VR.

Module 2: The Customer Journey and Technology

  • Understanding the intersections between online and offline channels to provide a seamless experience is known as omnichannel retail.
  • Using mobile apps for tailored marketing, loyalty schemes, and product information is known as mobile engagement.
  • Self-service technologies: navigation apps, kiosks, and how they empower users.

Module 3: Interaction and Establishing Connections

  • Finding the needs of the consumer and going above and above with active listening and questioning strategies.
  • Efficient communication through phone, email, social media, and in-person meetings.
  • Increasing client loyalty: techniques to promote repeat business and trust.
  • Exceptional service to a varied audience requires cultural sensitivity and inclusivity.

Module 4: Case Studies and Role-Playing to Show Innovation in Action

  • Examining innovative retail strategies and drawing insights from leaders in the field who adopt novel technologies.
  • Live-action role-playing games: Practice implementing creative solutions to actual client scenarios.
  • Creating a cutting-edge approach to customer service: Make a strategy for using technology to provide individualized service.

Final Assessment:

Interactive tests and homework: Assess the information you've learned during the course. Participation in role-playing: Assess customer service abilities in a real-world situation. Creating a cutting-edge customer care plan customized for a particular retail company is the last job.

Social media awareness and personal branding: creating a powerful online presence to engage with clients.

You will learn from this program with the abilities and know-how necessary to succeed as a customer service associate in the cutting-edge retail industry.
