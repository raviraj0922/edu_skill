Program Objectives:

  • Master the following cashier functions: product scanning, payment processing, and customer service.
  • Gain experience with next-generation POS systems and self-checkout terminals.
  • Understand the latest retail technology, such as mobile wallets, contactless payments, and omnichannel solutions.
  • Develop strong communication and conflict resolution skills to ensure a great shoppers experience.
  • Discover the best practices for inventory management, loss prevention, and data security and developing trends in retail.
